MAPL Application Overview

This online application information guides you through the steps required to apply to the MAPL Program

You will be asked to provide the following:

  • Demographic information, contact information including email address and phone numbers, test scores, recommendation information, educational background, and other pertinent information in order to fully assess the applicant's qualifications for program acceptance.
  • You will need to complete a Statement of Purpose than can be completed online or uploaded as an attachment. Please review the application in its entirety prior to beginning the process in order to become familiar with all the steps.

Once all application information is entered correctly and completely, your online application will be submitted to the MAPL Program Committee for their review. If any errors are found or if there is missing information, our office will contact you. We may also contact you for a personal interview as part of the admissions process.
